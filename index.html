Welcome to Aujla Farming, the largest buffalo farm in Ontario! With a steadfast commitment to quality and sustainability, we take pride in raising a thriving herd of water buffalo to produce premium dairy products. Our farm is a symbol of excellence, combining traditional farming methods with modern practices to deliver the finest milk and dairy products to our community. At Aujla Farming, we are passionate about supporting local agriculture and providing nutritious, farm-fresh products to families across Ontario.
