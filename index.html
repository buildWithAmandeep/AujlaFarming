Our Story

Growing dairy the slow, ethical way.

Aujla Farming began with a handful of water buffalo and a promise to deliver nutrient-dense milk without shortcuts. Today we manage rotational grazing, on-farm pasteurization, and cold-chain delivery that keeps every pour as pure as the pasture it came from.

  • Heritage water buffalo herd raised on native Ontario grasses.
  • Small-batch pasteurization locks in vitamins and flavour.
  • Reusable glass bottling and doorstep delivery across the GTA.
